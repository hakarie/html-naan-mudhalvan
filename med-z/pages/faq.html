Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What services does MED-Z offer?

We provide a range of healthcare services, including general consultations, diagnostics, surgeries, and emergency care.

2. How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our website by visiting the Appointment Page, or by calling our helpline.

3. What are the hospital's visiting hours?

Visiting hours are from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. On Sundays, visits are allowed from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

4. Do you accept health insurance?

Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans. Please contact our billing department for more details.

5. How can I contact MED-Z for emergencies?

For emergencies, please call our 24/7 helpline at +123-456-7890 or visit the emergency department directly.
