Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2025

1. Introduction

MED-Z is committed to protecting the privacy of our patients, visitors, and staff. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, date of birth, etc.
  • Medical Information: Medical history, treatment details, prescriptions, and insurance details.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our website (e.g., IP address, browser type).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide healthcare services and manage patient records.
  • To schedule and confirm appointments.
  • To communicate with you regarding your care and updates.
  • For internal analytics and service improvements.

4. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal and medical information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information with trusted third parties only for service delivery purposes (e.g., payment processing).

6. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Request a copy of your data.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns, please visit our Contact Page.
